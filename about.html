TED

TED is a global platform with an annual conference that brings together the world’s leading thinkers, social entrepreneurs and changemakers to share ideas that matter in any discipline—technology, entertainment, design, science, environment, business. The talks at the conference, “TED Talks,” are then made available for free online at TED.com. Over 100 million people view TED videos annually.

TEDxNITCalicut

TEDxNITCalicut was formed in the year 2012 by a group of students who were passionate about spreading ideas that change attitudes. Today we have an incredible team of extremely dedicated and talented volunteers who bring together some of the most inspiring thinkers of our local community to share their ideas with the world. NIT Calicut is hosting its 4th edition of TEDx this March. As a promotional event, we conducted an open mic at Design Ashram, Calicut in November. The event garnered participants from various backgrounds in and around Calicut and had a good turnout. This year a curated line-up of speakers and storytellers from across disciplines take to the stage to inspire an audience of thinkers and doers. In this daylong event, we are looking forward to feature a mix of visionary entrepreneurs, leaders, artists, writers, scientists and people who overcame overwhelming obstacles to create an extraordinary life, all on their own terms.
